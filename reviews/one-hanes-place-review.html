What is One Hanes Place?

One Hanes Place (OHP) is the official online outlet store for Hanesbrands Inc., one of the largest apparel companies in the world. Think of it as the digital equivalent of a Hanes outlet mall — same brands, same products, lower prices.

The site carries products from the entire Hanesbrands portfolio, which includes some of the most recognizable names in everyday apparel:

  • Hanes — Classic underwear, t-shirts, socks, and basics for the whole family
  • Bali — Women's bras and intimates known for comfort-focused designs
  • Playtex — Bras and shapewear, including the popular Playtex 18-Hour bra
  • Maidenform — Trendy bras, panties, and shapewear
  • Champion — Athletic wear, sweatshirts, and activewear
  • Barely There — Ultra-lightweight, seamless intimates
  • Wonderbra — Push-up and plunge bras
  • Just My Size — Plus-size intimates and apparel

The product range covers bras, panties, hosiery, shapewear, socks, t-shirts, and loungewear. If it's made by Hanesbrands, you'll likely find it here — often at a significant discount.

Pricing: How Much Can You Actually Save?

This is where One Hanes Place shines. The entire premise of the site is outlet pricing, and the discounts are genuine compared to buying the same products at retail stores or directly from brand websites.

Here's what we found during our research:

  • Everyday prices are typically 30–50% below retail
  • Clearance section regularly hits 60–72% off
  • BOGO sales (Buy One, Get One 50% Off) run frequently on bras
  • Newsletter signup gets you 20% off your first order
  • Holiday sales (Black Friday, Memorial Day, etc.) can push discounts even higher

For example, a Bali Comfort Revolution bra that retails for $44 at department stores typically sells for $22–28 on One Hanes Place. During clearance events, we've seen it drop to $15.

The pricing is straightforward — no gimmicks, no inflated "original" prices. These are legitimate outlet deals on genuine Hanesbrands products.

Product Quality: Same as Retail

One important thing to understand: these are the exact same products you'd find at Macy's, Kohl's, Target, or any other retailer that carries Hanesbrands products. One Hanes Place isn't selling seconds, irregulars, or lower-quality outlet-specific lines.

The products are first-quality, authentic Hanesbrands merchandise. The only difference is the price.

That said, quality expectations should match the brand. Hanes and Bali make solid everyday basics — comfortable, durable, and functional. They're not luxury lingerie. If you're looking for intricate lace details or premium fabrics, you'll want to look elsewhere. But for everyday comfort that holds up wash after wash, these brands deliver consistently.

Sizing: Know Before You Order

Sizing is one area where One Hanes Place could improve. The site carries products from multiple brands, each with its own sizing chart. Here's what you need to know:

  • Bali and Playtex tend to run true to size for most women
  • Maidenform can run slightly small, especially in band sizes
  • Hanes basics (tees, underwear) generally run true to size
  • Champion activewear runs slightly generous

Our advice: check the specific brand's size chart on the product page, not just the generic OHP chart. If you're between sizes in bras, consider going up a band size and down a cup size (the "sister size" trick). And take advantage of the generous return policy if something doesn't fit.

Shipping: The Biggest Drawback

If there's one consistent complaint about One Hanes Place, it's shipping speed. This is the weakest part of the experience.

  • Free shipping on orders with 2+ bras (no minimum dollar amount)
  • Standard shipping starts at $6.99 for other orders
  • Delivery time is typically 7–14 business days for standard shipping
  • Expedited options are available but can be pricey

Multiple customer reviews mention slow shipping — some reporting 2–3 week delivery times. If you need something quickly, One Hanes Place is not the place to order. Plan ahead and treat it like an outlet shopping trip: the savings are worth the wait, but you're not getting next-day delivery.

Returns: Surprisingly Generous

One Hanes Place offers a 45-day return policy for unused items, which is better than many online retailers. Here's how it works:

  • Return unused items within 45 days of purchase
  • Free return shipping with a prepaid return label
  • $6.95 deducted from your refund if you use their return label
  • Refunds issued to your original form of payment

The 45-day window gives you plenty of time to try things on and decide. The prepaid label makes returns hassle-free, even if there's a small deduction. Overall, the return policy is fair and customer-friendly.

Great for:
